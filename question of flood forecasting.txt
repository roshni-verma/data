4.How do different factors like "Urbanization" and "Deforestation" impact "Flood Probability"?
Urbanization" aur "Deforestation" jaise factors "Flood Probability" ko kaise prabhavit karte hain, iska matlab hai ki yeh samajhna ki shehron ka vikas (urbanization) aur pedon ka katna (deforestation) kis tarah se baadh aane ki sambhavana ko badhate hain.

Urbanization (Shehri Vikas)
Badhte Hue Pakke Satah: Jab shehr badhte hain, to zameen par concrete, road, aur buildings ban jaate hain. Yeh pakke satah paani ko zameen mein samane nahi dete, jisse zyada paani surface par bahta hai aur baadh ki sambhavana badh jaati hai.

Prakritik Nalaon ki Badalti Huyi Vyavastha: Shehron mein vaakya prakritik nala aur nadiyon ko badal kar artificial drainage system banaya jaata hai. Agar yeh system achhi tarah se design nahi kiya gaya, to yeh tez baarish ko sambhal nahi paata, jisse shehr mein jal bharaav (urban flooding) ho sakta hai.

Floodplain par Nirman: Urbanization ke wajah se prakritik floodplains, jo adhik paani ko samane aur rakhne mein madad karte hain, un par nirman ho jata hai. Isse prakritik roop se baadh ko rokne ki kshamata kam ho jaati hai.

Deforestation (Vanon ki Katai)
Pedon ka Khatma: Ped aur paudhe zameen mein paani ko absorb karte hain aur surface runoff ko kam karte hain. Jab ped kat diye jaate hain, to zyada paani zameen par bahta hai, jisse baadh ki sambhavana badh jaati hai.

Mittiko ka Khatma (Soil Erosion): Ped mitti ko jod kar rakhte hain. Jab ped kat jaate hain, to mitti ka erode hona shuru ho jaata hai, jo nadiyon aur nalaon ko bhar deta hai. Isse nadiyan aur nala apna paani le jaane ki kshamata kho dete hain, jisse baadh aane ka khatra badh jaata hai.

Jal Chakra ka Badlav: Van jal chakra mein mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhate hain, jismein paani vayu mandal mein evaporate hota hai. Vanon ki katai se yeh jal chakra badal jaata hai, jisse baarish ke pattern mein badlav ho sakta hai aur zyada extremes weather events aa sakte hain, jo baadh ka khatra badhate hain.

Combined Impact (Sanyukt Prabhav)
Urbanization aur deforestation agar saath-saath hote hain, to inka mil-jul kar asar aur bhi zyada badh jaata hai:

Zyada Surface Runoff: Urban areas mein pakke satah aur deforested areas mein kam ped hone ki wajah se zyada surface runoff hota hai, jo drainage systems aur prakritik jalashayon ko overload kar deta hai.

Prakritik Rokho ka Khatma: Urbanization aur deforestation dono prakritik rookho ko kam kar dete hain, jisse tez baarish ke dauran baadh aane ki sambhavana badh jaati hai.

In simple terms, "Urbanization" aur "Deforestation" dono hi factors baadh aane ki sambhavana ko badhate hain, kyunki yeh prakritik taur par paani ko sambhalne aur rokne ki kshamata ko kam kar dete hain.
